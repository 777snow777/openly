welcome to openly, an album I made in 2023 about the spirals between grief and growth, loss and emergence, dreams and memories. these spiral have always been paths of returning to myself.
making this album has been a release ritual, a slow practice of letting go. I wrote these poems in moments over the past 4 years that I could find stillness inside motion - in my sleep, in parked cars, in line at the post office, waiting for the bus, sitting next to the Pacific Ocean.
